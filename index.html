🎌JAPAN🗾

I am the creator of this webpage and as a dream destination of mine, I chose to visit the Asian archipelago, Japan.



The island nation located in Eastern Asia known as the "Land of the Rising Sun" is a country known for it its vast countrylands + mountanius terrain overall making up for its beautiful natural features as shown below. The archipelago was first visited by people about 30,000 years ago, at that time, the main islands were connected to mainland Siberia. All in all Japan is an island nation filled to the brim with large amounts of culture, beautiful places of nature that many love (or would love) to visit.
